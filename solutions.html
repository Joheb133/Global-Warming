Solutions to Global Warming

To tackle global warming we are told to reduce our carbon footprint by opting for cleaner energy's like solar and wind, or cycle to school instead of driving. Although this is a step in the right direction, it's too small as step because only some people will make an effort to change their lifestyle.

Governments need to take action and enforce stricter laws in order to make an actual change to the problem. Countries should invest in technologies to improve their electrical infrastructure, the main focus being moving away from non-renewable resources to renewables.

Thing's may seem bleak however when people come together we can make a real impact. At the end of 2019, a Youtuber known as MrBeast online, started an online campaign to raise $20 million before the year ends to celebrate his channel reaching 20 million subscribers.

Australia is currently burning and the brave firefighters are trying to put this horror to an end. This fire has killed 1 billion animals and 25 people so far. Again the internet has come together but in an unconventional way. An Instagram model tweeted she'd sell her nudes for donations to Australia. As of 6th January she has raised $1 million.
